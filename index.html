About Me

I'm a public-health data professional with 15+ years of experience working on epidemiological research and data-for-impact initiatives in the global health space. My work focuses on turning complex health data into actionable insights that improve lives worldwide.

Curiosity, empathy, and collaboration guide my approach. I'm currently exploring Generative AI to automate analysis, streamline reporting, and amplify knowledge sharing in global health.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8-week live online certification offered by the GRAPH Network. The programme teaches no-code AI tools for automating content creation, data analysis, knowledge management, and routine workflows.

Key modules include:

  • Documents, Presentations & Websites
  • Automated Data Analysis
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis
  • Custom GPTs and Chatbots
  • AI Workflows for Task Automation
  • GenAI for Communication & Outreach
  • Capstone Project & Showcase
